About Lucky 8 Lawn

Founded in 2015, Lucky 8 Lawn has been providing top-quality lawn care services to the Vancouver community for nearly a decade. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted lawn care providers in the region.

Our team of experienced professionals takes pride in every project we undertake. We believe that a well-maintained lawn enhances not just your property's curb appeal, but also your quality of life.

We're fully licensed and insured, and we stand behind all of our work with a satisfaction guarantee. When you choose Lucky 8 Lawn, you're choosing peace of mind knowing your outdoor space is in good hands.
